## Story

**As a** network admin,
**I want** to manage group policies for client devices,
**so that** I can apply bandwidth limits and access rules to groups.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `discover_group_policies` returns all group policies with bandwidth limits and scheduling
2. `configure_group_policy` supports creating/updating policies with bandwidth, firewall, and scheduling rules
3. Full CRUD for group policies
4. Backup created before any group policy config change
5. Integration into `full_discovery()` output
6. Natural language: "create a group policy with 10Mbps limit" works
